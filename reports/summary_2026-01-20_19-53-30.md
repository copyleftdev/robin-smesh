### **2. Cryptocurrency Fraud is a Secondary Revenue Stream**
- **Bitcoin Exploiter** and **USDT Flash Program** are **high-risk scams** targeting crypto users.

- **Evidence:**

### **4. Social Engineering is the Primary Attack Vector**

  - **"Ruining somebody’s life or clearing your debts"** (appeals to desperation).

### **5. The Vendor is Likely a Low-Tier Cybercriminal Group**
